ABOUT

I started ballet and tap classes at Edge Dance studio in Murphy, Texas. After practicing ballet and tap for a while, our studio had the first annual recital for our dance batch. Finally, after a year of dancing ballet and tap, I enrolled in Natya Nidi dance academy in Murphy, Texas. Soon, after settling in and practicing, my kuchipudi batch and I celebrated our annual dance recital at a classical dance studio in Plano. After a few years, we had our 2nd recital at the same location. 4 years passed and I had been in the same dance academy. When my family decided to move to Allen, TX, I had to join a different dance class due to distance and time.
